# Women’s Weekend Self‑Defence Course: Empowering Women in Santacruz  

OliveHorse Fitness Academy provides Karate, fitness and practical self‑defence programmes. These programmes are intended for children, teenagers, adults and women, and the academy is positioned in Santacruz, Mumbai.  

## Course Overview  
This short self‑defence course focuses on practical techniques that help participants stay safe in everyday situations. As part of the academy’s self‑defence offering, the training emphasizes real‑world applications rather than theory.  

## What to Expect  
- Practical self‑defence instruction drawn from the academy’s established programmes.  
- Elements of fitness and basic karate movements that support overall confidence and readiness.  
- A supportive environment where women can learn and practice skills at their own pace.  

## Who Can Join  
Because the academy’s programmes are open to children, teenagers, adults and women, this course is suitable for women seeking to build personal‑safety abilities.  

## Location  
The training takes place at OliveHorse Fitness Academy’s centre in Santacruz, Mumbai.
